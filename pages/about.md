## About the Collection

This collection features a variety of vintage makeup advertisements spanning most of the 20th century. It provides insight into popular makeup products and cosmetics companies, beauty trends, cultural and aesthetic values, and advertising styles. The collection may be of interest to fashion history students, historians, cosmetelogy students, and anyone wishing to learn more about the beauty industry in the 1900s.
